About Donald Maloney

Donald Maloney is a law student at George Washington University Law School, set to graduate in 2023. He is passionate about tax law and has developed a keen interest in international tax and cross-border transactions.

Throughout his time at GW Law School, Donald has been committed to learning the intricacies of tax law and its impact on global economics. His blog serves as a platform for sharing his insights and knowledge on various topics related to economics and tax law, as well as a way to engage with other professionals and enthusiasts in the field.
